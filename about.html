    About Me

    My Story

    I’m half English and half Malaysian Indian, but grew up moving around the world – including Nepal, Kenya, USA, Egypt, Switzerland and the UK. Having recently moved to Lisbon, Portugal from Bristol, I continue to enjoy travel and have a diverse background which has inspired an interest in people, culture, art and design from all corners of the globe.

    Outside of my passion for creative in all shapes and forms, I am also a lover of adventure and being outdoors. I am a sunseeker, and find a lot of peace from nature – a swim in the ocean or a hike in the mountains or forest fills my cup up more than most things. I am a proud introvert, and will happy fill up and enjoy my own space and time, while also loving people and spending time with those close to me. Some good music and a night out for a dance is always good for the soul too.

    My professional background is in content publishing, marketing, communications and design. I have worked in-house in small and large marketing teams, and more recently have been drawn to the world of freelance in content publishing and UI design. My multicultural background has paved a good base for both of these things – in that I am observant and have the ability to empathise with people in different situations. This helps when writing copy, conducting interviews, writing up case studies and thinking up designs.
