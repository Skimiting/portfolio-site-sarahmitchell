My Work

NOVA

NOVA is an e-commerce app created to empower individuals of all backgrounds to embrace their unique beauty through high-quality, sustainable and ethically sourced makeup. Users can browse an inventory that can be filtered in a variety of ways depending on their needs. NOVA allows them to purchase their favourite inclusive and sustainable makeup easily from at home, or while on the go.

Seasoned

Seasoned is a responsive recipe web app that focuses on female hormonal health. The product helps users find and plan recipes that support their hormonal health at different types of their monthly cycle, and throughout their different life stages. The product keeps users engaged across a variety of platforms, and it allows for intuitive and single-minded navigation, personalisation and users will also be incentivised by tracking their progress.
